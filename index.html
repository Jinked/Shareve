The ShaReve Solution

The next-gen crowdfunding platform that transforms how people invest in innovation, business, and ideas.

You don’t invest in a company.
You invest in its success — in the income, revenue, or financial return that a project actually generates.

01.

Projects Launch on SHAREVE

Entrepreneurs, professionals, or individuals propose projects with clear income expectations — from product sales to royalties, salaries, or services.

02.

Income-Based Agreements

Instead of shares or debt, each project offers investors a defined percentage of future income for a set period — for example:
“Invest €50,000 and receive 30% of the turnover for 5 years.”

03.

AI-Powered Deal Calculator

SHAREVE’s proprietary Deal Calculator uses AI and financial modeling to determine a fair “How Much in Exchange for What” (HMEW) ratio, balancing risks and rewards for both parties.

04.

Smart Contracts & Tokenization

Each deal is secured, traceable, and stored on blockchain. The agreement can be tokenized, creating a digital asset that can be traded anytime, giving investors liquidity and exit options.

05.

Trade & Grow

Tokens representing revenue shares can be traded on SHAREVE’s own marketplace, making every investment negotiable, transferable, and transparent.

A Revolution in Crowdfunding

Traditional crowdfunding has limits. Complex procedures, opaque results, lack of liquidity, and no control make it inefficient and risky.

SHAREVE eliminates these barriers with a new model that is:

  • Simpler and more transparent
  • Less expensive and faster
  • Flexible for any type of project
  • Traceable, negotiable, and liquid
